# Recipe 1

GRILLS NOODLES

Contens

  • Time: 20 Munites
  • Sever: Alone

Element Quanlity
Fresh shrimp 30
Noodles 100 Gr
Lemongrass 20 gr
Sprouts 25 Gr
Moon cinnamon 3 leaf
Sesame Oil 1 Small spoon
Coffee Chicken 1 Fruit
Salt 1 Spoon
Sweet 1/2 Spoon
Scallion 10 Gr
Tool grill 1
plate 1

The step cooking Grill Noodle

  • Step 1 :

    • Shrimps peeled, removed the head, cut back to draw black thread. The first part of shrimp is not thrown away,
    but set aside in a bowl

  • Step 2:

    • Lemongrass stripped the outer leaves, cut the roots and crushed. Boil 1 pot of water about 3 liters, add the
    shrimp, lemongrass and bay leaves and boil over medium heat for about 1 hour. If you have a pressure cooker,
    you can save time cooking shrimp from this step. After the cooking time, you will see the shrimp broth is
    less than it was originally.

  • Step 3:

    • Prepare a small pot, drop the eggs to boil for about 6 minutes and take out. Eggs are peeled, cut into halves
    or quarters.

  • Step 4:

    • Prepare a large pot, pour the shrimp broth over, then add tofu, shrimp and noodles to cook.

  • Step 5:

    • Put the noodles in the bowl, add bean sprouts, chives, eggs, a few pieces of beans and shrimp. Top place cilantro
    , sprinkle with roasted peanuts, scallions and a little sesame oil.

    # Recipe 2

    FAST NOODLES

    Contens

    • Time: 5 munites
    • Sever: alone

    Element quanlity
    Noodles 100g
    Vegertable 50g
    Hot water 200 ml
    Spoon 1
    Plate 1

    The step cooking Fast Noodle

  • Step 1 :

    • Boil water with instant noodles. Blanch the noodles, to filter the gray matter as well as the fried noodles.
    When the noodles are separated and cooked evenly, pour the boiling water and pour the noodles into the bowl.

  • Step 2 :

    • Boil another pot of boiling water, pour the noodles in the bowl and return to the pot of water. Quickly
    turn off the stove to not break noodles. Then, you add the seasoning noodles. If you want to eat dry noodles,
    you can remove the noodles and mix them with seasoning packages as usual.

  • Step 3 :

    • If you want to eat more eggs or meat, fish, shrimp, green vegetables ... then you should prepare them separately
    and add to noodles. It is best to add about 150 g of green vegetables such as cabbage, cauliflower, broccoli,
    bean sprouts ... Adding vegetables to the meal will cause a large amount of fat to be carried out into the body.
    From there, it will minimize the main harm caused by instant noodles.
