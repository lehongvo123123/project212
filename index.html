  2. The Think very intereting in Sapa

    3. - Small village

    A whole new experience for you about daily life and outstanding cultural features of the ethnic
    minorities in Sapa. Amidst the breathtaking beauty of the rice fields, small local villages appear,
    creating great highlights for the beauty of Sapa. In Sapa, there are sublime terraced fields,

    -Mountain trip in Midle village

    Along with mountain trekking, Fansipan is an activity not to be missed in your Sapa trip. From the
    top of Fansipan, you can admire the wild beauty of the mountains of Northwest Vietnam and the
    fanciful white clouds surrounding the mountain.

    -Pu Long Nature Reserve

    Pu Luong is one of the largest nature reserves in Vietnam located in Thanh Hoa province, a region
    of central Vietnam. It has a high level of biodiversity, especially more than 150 species of
    butterflies and nearly 600 animals that you can have an unforgettable experience. If you love
    thrills,you can join a trekking tour to the top of Pu Luong, 1700 meters high above sea level.
    Or you can explore the outstanding cultures of the ethnic minorities in this beautiful

    -Sapa rose valley

    It is one of the largest flower gardens in the Northwest region. Sapa Rose Valley has more than 1
    million roses with many different colors and flowers are extremely large. This is also one of the
    most romantic places in Sapa

    -Sapa rock church

    Sa Pa Stone Church is located in the heart of Sapa town, built in 1895. It is a Roman Gotic architecture
    with arches, spiers, bell towers, ... This is the last remaining work. from the French colonial period
    but still intact.
